Features

Task Planning

Failure to plan is often a plan to fail. As it is the case with Agile software development, planning out the tasks for a sprint is crucial in the delivery of the project. The Limber team knows this for a fact, from our 15 years cumulative experience in Agile software development. Limber allows for your agile team to break down a project into small individual task with their constituent task as well as time estimates, helping you have a much more clearer plan.

Task Iterations

Agile software development may involve multiple teams in remote locations working on the same project; we understand that. Limber provides for organization registration where teams can be added under a project. The central access to the project allows for faster and more effective iterations over the task created in the project across all the teams in the organization. Teams can plan their tasks taking into consideration of the tasks other teams are working on helping minimize blockers that may arise from depedencies across the various teams.

Task Delivery

Customer is key when it comes to Agile software development. Limber employs time estimations when creating task which gives the projects stakeholders a clear picture of the progress of the project. This is crucial information that helps reflect when tasks and the project in general can be delivered.
